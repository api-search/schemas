A trust policy specifies which OCI registries and artifacts are trusted for verification, which trust stores contain the valid signing certificates, and what signature verification level to apply. The trust policy is evaluated by Notation during the 'notation verify' operation to determine whether a signature on a container image or OCI artifact is acceptable.
