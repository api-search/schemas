description: A Route defines rules to match client requests. Each Route is associated with a Service, and a Service may have multiple Routes. When a request matches a Route, Kong proxies it to the associated Service. Routes support matching on hosts, paths, methods, headers, SNIs, and sources/destinations for stream routing.
